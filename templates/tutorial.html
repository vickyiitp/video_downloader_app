How It Works

Using RapidGrab is simple! This guide explains how to download from both public and private websites.

Downloading from Public Sites (YouTube, Facebook, etc.)

  1. Find the video you want to download on a public site like YouTube.
  2. Copy the video's URL from your browser's address bar.
  3. Paste the URL into the input box on the RapidGrab homepage.
  4. A preview of the video will appear. Click "Confirm Download" to start.

Downloading from Private Sites (University Portals, SharePoint, etc.)

Private videos require you to prove you have permission to view them. You do this by providing your browser's login cookies.

  1. Install a browser extension called "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" for Chrome or Firefox.
  2. Log in to the private website (e.g., your university portal) as you normally would.
  3. Navigate to the exact page where you can play the private video.
  4. Click the cookie extension's icon in your browser and click "Export". This saves a cookies.txt file.
  5. Open the downloaded cookies.txt file with a text editor, select all the text (Ctrl+A), and copy it (Ctrl+C).
  6. On RapidGrab, paste the video URL in the top box and the cookie data you just copied into the larger box below it.
  7. The preview will appear. Click "Confirm Download" to get your private video.
