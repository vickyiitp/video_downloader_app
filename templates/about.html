About RapidGrab

RapidGrab was created to be a simple, powerful, and user-friendly tool for downloading online video content for offline use. Whether you're a student saving a lecture, a researcher archiving a resource, or just someone who wants to watch a video on the go, RapidGrab provides a seamless solution.

Our application is built on a robust Python and Django backend, ensuring fast and reliable processing, and a modern, responsive frontend for a smooth user experience on any device.

Our Mission

We believe in providing a straightforward utility that respects user privacy. All processing is done securely, and any temporary data, such as login cookies for private videos, is deleted from our servers immediately after use.
